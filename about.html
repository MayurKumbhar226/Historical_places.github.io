About

Ajanta & Ellora Caves, Gateway of India, Shaniwar Vada, Chattrapati Shivaji Terminus, Aga Khan Palace, Elephant Caves, Raigad Fort, Jaigadh Fort, Lohagadh Fort, Kolaba Fort, Visapur Fort, Bibi ka Maqbara, Rajabai Clock Tower, Lenyadri Caves, Korlai Fort, Chand Minar, Wai Ghat and Temples and many more. Historical Places in Maharashtra are plenty and will take you back in times giving your holiday the much required thrill. If you love architectural brilliance and want to have a unique experience, then visiting the Gateway of India should be ideal for you. And while you are at it, you can visit the famous Elephanta Caves, too. It has been tagged as a World Heritage Site recently and is located pretty close to the Gateway of India as well. Aside from the well-established architecture, the monuments of Maharashtra are worth visiting too! As an aficionado, you will love the constructional brilliance of Chand Minar, which is located near Daulatabad. In addition to it, the Bibi ka Maqbara is also a must-visit for you, if you are visiting the city for the first time. This beautiful monument has a striking resemblance to the Taj Mahal and, therefore, offers a sublime vibe of romanticism to the visitors!
